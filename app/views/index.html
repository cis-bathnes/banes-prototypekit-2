Before you start

Make sure that the child is living in Bath and North East Somerset. You can do this by finding their local council.

To complete your referral make sure you have:

  • the name, date of birth and address for each child you are including in the referral
  • the name and contact details for each parent or carer
  • information about what support has been offered to the family

With this information to hand your referral should take about X minutes to complete in total.

To report a concern about a child who already has a social worker, you should contact the social worker or unit involved.

For more information about our thresholds for assessment, please refer to the Threshold for Assessment document.
